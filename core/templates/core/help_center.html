FAQs

Q: I want help with Returns and Refunds +

→ I want to change the address for delivery of my order. Is it possible now? +

The delivery address for your order can be changed depending on its status. Please check your Order Details section to see if the change option is available.

→ I want to cancel my return request. What should I do? +

You can cancel your return request from your Orders page before it’s processed by our team. Once the pickup is arranged, cancellation may not be possible.

Q: I want to manage my orders +

You can manage all your orders by visiting the My Orders section in your profile. There you can:
  • Track your shipments
  • Cancel or modify orders
  • Request returns or refunds
  • Contact support for issues

Q: I have a payment issue +

If the amount has been deducted but order not confirmed, it usually gets refunded automatically within 5–7 business days. For faster resolution, contact our support team via the Contact Us page.
